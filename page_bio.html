Jamey Dunn

The Man, the Myth, the Legend

It's safe to say that for someone who based their life plan off a fever dream as a fourteen year old, I've stuck to my guns surprisingly well. I committed with myself to become a successful video game developer, though the thought had never crossed my mind prior.
Now, six years later, I can finally present myself as such. I am a member of the development team in the GIMM department (Games, Interactive Media, and Mobile) at Boise State University, and am currently pursuing a Bachelor's Degree in Video Game Development.
I have multiple personal projects in the works, ranging from games, music, personal artwork, and other various undertakings in areas you'll see here on this collective website.
